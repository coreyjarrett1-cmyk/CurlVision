# **App Name**: CurlVision AI

## Core Features:

- AI Hair Analysis & Profile: Utilizes multimodal computer vision (Gemini/Claude API) to analyze uploaded hair images, determining curl pattern (3C to 4C), porosity, and density to create a personalized hair profile.
- Personal Style Vault: Allows users to upload, log, and organize images of their past hairstyles, serving as a visual diary stored securely in Firestore.
- AI Style Recommendation Tool: An AI-powered tool that suggests new hairstyles tailored to the user's hair profile (analyzed via computer vision) and their accumulated style preferences from the vault.
- Hair Health Regimen Tracker: Enables users to track and log hair care activities such as wash days, deep conditioning treatments, and trim schedules for better hair health management using Firestore.
- AI Protective Style Alerts: Leverages AI to monitor the duration of protective styles based on user input, sending proactive notifications for takedown and scalp treatments to prevent damage.
- Curated Hair Culture Hub: A sleek, magazine-style blog providing curated articles, trend insights, and scientifically-backed educational content focused on diverse hair care and culture, updated via RAG from the Gemini/Claude API.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary Color: A vibrant golden yellow (#F4C025) chosen for its celebratory, luxurious, and warm aesthetic, standing out against a dark backdrop.
- Background Color: A deep, rich charcoal with a subtle golden undertone (#221E10), maintaining visual continuity with the primary color in a sophisticated dark scheme.
- Accent Color: A bold, warm burnt orange (#E65A17) that creates striking contrast and adds visual interest, providing an energetic complement to the primary golden hue.
- Body and Headline Font: 'Inter' (sans-serif), chosen for its modern, clean lines and excellent readability, contributing to a high-end, contemporary magazine aesthetic.
- Icons: Utilize 'Material Symbols Outlined' for crisp, clear, and universally recognizable iconography, maintaining consistency and enhancing user experience.
- Layout: A mobile-first, single-column design with a max-width container, emphasizing visual content and spaciousness, ideal for a focused, high-end app experience.
- Animations: Incorporate subtle and deliberate animations, such as glowing indicators for active states, gentle transitions on hovers, and minimal interactive button feedback, for a refined and engaging user interface.
